# Color Script

Purpose: control emotional color progression across the full video.

## Color States

| Color State | Palette | Emotion | Automatic Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cold Fear | Cool gray, desaturated blue, restrained skin warmth | Anxiety, threat, uncertainty | Headlines, risk, loss, isolation |
| Neutral Thinking | Balanced gray, natural skin, low saturation | Analysis, reflection, explanation | Reframe, research, consideration |
| Warm Discovery | Soft warm neutrals, natural amber practicals | Curiosity, insight, human connection | Mechanism understood, new possibility |
| Blue Focus | Clean muted blue accents, neutral base | Precision, discipline, forward motion | Planning, building, operations |
| Amber Confidence | Warm amber practicals, deeper contrast | Control, conviction, competence | Decision, strategy, founder momentum |
| Golden Opportunity | Warm highlights, restrained gold, open shadows | Achievement, possibility, payoff | Final transformation, sunrise, success |
| Dark Reflection | Deep neutral shadows, practical pools of light | Sacrifice, uncertainty, consequence | Late-night work, private decision |

## Automatic Shift Rules

- Start with the color state that matches the script's initial emotion.
- Assign each script block a deliberate color state or gradual transition that reflects its chapter.
- Shift color only at a script block boundary or script-defined emotional turn.
- Move from cold to neutral when fear becomes analysis.
- Move from neutral to warm when the mechanism becomes clear.
- Use blue focus during disciplined action, not as a default tech color.
- Use amber confidence when a person gains control.
- Reserve golden opportunity for genuine payoff.
- Use dark reflection before resolution, not as an ending unless the story is unresolved.
- Keep skin tones natural through every state.
- Never use neon gradients, cyberpunk color, or uncontrolled teal-orange grading.
- Preserve light direction and architectural continuity across evolving block master images.
- Do not use later payoff lighting in an earlier fear or thinking block.

## Example Arc

```text
Cold Fear -> Neutral Thinking -> Blue Focus -> Amber Confidence -> Golden Opportunity
```
