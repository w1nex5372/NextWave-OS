# Production Package - Video 01

## 1. Production Summary

- Video title: The Biggest Opportunity In AI Is Not Automation
- Target length: 60 seconds
- Format: vertical 9:16
- Core promise: AI's biggest business impact is not replacing workers; it is redesigning how work moves through a company.
- Viewer takeaway: The next software winners will coordinate decisions and handoffs, not just add AI features.
- Visual style: premium Netflix-style business documentary with Apple/Google/Microsoft corporate realism.
- Tool targets: HeyGen, image generation, Hailuo/Kling/Runway/Pika/Minimax, CapCut/DaVinci

## 6. Editing Plan

- Timeline rhythm: fast hook, measured middle, confident payoff; cuts every 2-4 seconds after the opening.
- Caption style: clean modern sans-serif, white lower-third, one muted blue highlight per key phrase.
- Transition style: straight cuts and match cuts, one clean push transition into the mechanism block.
- Music arc: low curiosity pulse, slight tension in problem, focused rhythm in mechanism, warm lift in payoff.
- SFX approach: minimal soft hits, UI taps, and room tone only.
- Color grade: clean corporate documentary, natural skin, soft contrast, no neon.
