# Editing

Editing must feel premium, fast, and clear.

## Timeline Defaults

- Format: 9:16.
- Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum, 4K vertical preferred.
- Frame rate: 24fps or 30fps.
- Hook density: visual change every 1-2 seconds for the first 8 seconds.
- Main body density: visual change every 2-4 seconds.
- Avoid cuts that feel random. Each cut must advance the idea.

## Captions

- Use clean white or off-white captions.
- Keep captions to 1-2 lines.
- Highlight only the key word or number when useful.
- No bouncing karaoke effects.
- No excessive emojis.
- No decorative caption boxes unless brand style requires it.

Caption style:

```text
Font: clean modern sans-serif.
Weight: semi-bold.
Placement: lower third, above platform UI safe zone.
Color: white or warm white.
Highlight: muted blue, graphite, or restrained green for key terms.
Shadow: soft dark shadow for readability.
```

## Text-On-Screen

Use text-on-screen for:

- Hook claim.
- Named concept.
- Key number.
- Turning point.
- Final takeaway.

Do not write full paragraphs on screen.

## Transitions

Use:

- Straight cuts.
- Match cuts.
- Subtle push transitions.
- Light motion blur between related shots.
- Clean dip to black only for major reset.

Avoid:

- Glitch transitions.
- Flash spam.
- Spin transitions.
- Cheap zoom punches.
- Random wipes.

## Music

Use restrained business-documentary music:

- Intro: quiet tension or curiosity.
- Problem: low pulse, muted synth, light percussion.
- Evidence: focused rhythm, subtle momentum.
- Payoff: warmer lift, restrained confidence.
- Outro: clean resolution.

Avoid epic trailer music unless the script is truly dramatic.

## SFX

Use minimal SFX:

- Soft whoosh for premium transitions.
- Subtle tap/click for UI closeups.
- Low impact for key reveal.
- Light room tone under office scenes.

Avoid noisy social-media SFX.

## Export Checklist

- 9:16 vertical.
- 1080x1920 or 2160x3840.
- H.264 or H.265.
- Captions inside safe zones.
- No text clipped by platform UI.
- Audio peaks below -1 dB.
- Dialogue clear above music.
- First frame is visually clear and intentional.
