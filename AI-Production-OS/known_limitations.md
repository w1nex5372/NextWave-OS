# Known Limitations

- The OS creates production instructions and prompts; it does not automatically generate images or videos.
- Generated visuals still require human review for faces, hands, screens, text artifacts, and continuity drift.
- Image-to-video tools may ignore some continuity instructions, so every rendered shot needs review against the block master image.
- Exact avatar identity depends on the avatar platform and available presenter settings.
- Scripts with many locations may need more blocks, which can increase production time.
- Legal, medical, financial, or brand-sensitive claims still need separate fact-checking before production.
