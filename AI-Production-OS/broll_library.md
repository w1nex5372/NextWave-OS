## B-Roll Rules

- Choose B-roll by story purpose, not keyword matching alone.
- Prefer observed action over posed action.
- Use a person, trace of a person, or consequence of human action whenever possible.
- Do not insert server rooms, skylines, or drones merely because the topic is technology or business.
- Keep screens under one-third of total visual time unless the script is specifically about interface evidence.
